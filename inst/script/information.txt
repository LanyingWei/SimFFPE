* example1.bam and example2.bam:
Both samples are from formalin-fixed paraffin-embedded (FFPE) tissues. example1.bam is 
sub-sampled from WGS data of patient with papillary tumors of the pineal region 
(Illumina HiSeq). example2.bam is sub-sampled from project PRJNA301548 of the 
European Nucleotide Archive (ENA) (https://www.ebi.ac.uk/ena/browser/view/PRJNA301548)

* example1.bam.bai and example2.bam.bai:
The indexes were generated using SAMtools.

* example.fasta:
Small example regions of 24 chromosomes from hg19 reference chromosome.

* PhredScoreProfile1.txt and PhredScoreProfile2.txt:
Examples of Phred score profiles generated by the function estimateSimParam of this 
package (with read length of 150 and 100 bp).

* regionsBam.txt and regionsSim.txt
Example regions covered by the example2.bam and example exonic regions of the example 
reference file.
